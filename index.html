Trip plans built from real places — on your phone, not in the cloud.

Joru plans your trip with an AI that runs on your device. Places, area guides and weather come from open data; the budget, sequence and transport are computed in code. The model only arranges the day and writes the guide — so it never invents a restaurant, a price or an opening time.

Why it's different

Not a chatbot guessing an itinerary.

Most AI planners let a model hallucinate places and prices. Joru splits the work so the facts stay real and the numbers stay reproducible.

Sources

Facts from open data

Real places, coordinates and area guides come from OpenStreetMap, Wikivoyage and Open-Meteo — never from the model's memory.

Deterministic

Numbers are computed

Budget, sequence, transport and validation run in plain Kotlin on your device. Same trip in, same plan out — every time.

On-device

AI only arranges & writes

The local model picks and orders among real candidates and drafts the guide. It can't add a place, a time or a price that wasn't there.

A look inside

Your whole trip, on one screen.

A day-by-day plan with real weather and prices, an interactive map of your stops, and a budget that tracks what you actually spend against the estimate.

Pricing

Free to plan. Premium when you want more.

Planning is free, with no account required — because the AI runs on your device, generating a plan costs nothing to run. Premium unlocks deeper, multi-city trips.
